This is a 2 player card game

Total 52 cards in the Deck, please click on to shuffle the deck and each player will have 26 shuffled cards.

How this game is played, each player will compare cards one by one. who has larger value will win. Lossing player will lost that card and go to next one.

At the end the player left with most the cards will win.

To restart the game you can sclick on shuffle button.
